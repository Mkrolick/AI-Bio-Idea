# Validation Checks - Direct Protein Synthesis

## Originality
- [ ] Searched for existing template-free protein synthesis approaches
- [ ] Reviewed current SPPS limitations and recent advances
- [ ] Checked if any startups/labs are already pursuing this exact approach
- [ ] Identified the novel technical contribution beyond "make SPPS better"

## Technical Tractability
- [ ] Chemistry for peptide bond formation at scale is established
- [ ] Proposed mechanism for handling full-length proteins (>300 aa) is plausible
- [ ] Folding strategy is addressed (co-translational folding analog, chaperones, etc.)
- [ ] Post-translational modifications can be incorporated or aren't needed
- [ ] Cost per protein is competitive with expression systems

## Clarity & Completeness
- [ ] Idea can be explained in 2-3 paragraphs
- [ ] Specific synthesis mechanism described
- [ ] Speed improvement quantified
- [ ] Key risks acknowledged with mitigations

## Competition Readiness
- [ ] Passes the "would the judges find this boring?" test
- [ ] Proposes a specific, concrete technical approach
- [ ] Addresses a widespread need (protein production)
- [ ] Not just "better SPPS" without a specific innovation
