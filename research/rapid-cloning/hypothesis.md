# Hypothesis: Rapid Gene Cloning via Cell-Free Assembly

## Core Idea
Replace the multi-day cloning workflow (restriction digest, ligation, transformation, colony screening) with a single-pot cell-free system that assembles, amplifies, and validates constructs in hours instead of days.

## Why This Matters
Gene cloning is one of the most universal wet-lab operations. Nearly every molecular biology experiment starts with cloning. The current workflow takes 3-5 days minimum, involves multiple hands-on steps, and has a ~50-80% success rate depending on the construct.

## Key Technical Questions
- Can cell-free TX-TL systems be optimized for construct validation without living cells?
- What assembly chemistries could work in a single-pot format?
- How do you verify correct assembly without transformation and colony picking?
