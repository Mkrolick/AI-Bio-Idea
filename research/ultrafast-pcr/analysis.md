# Technical Feasibility Analysis - Ultrafast PCR

## Approach Details
Photonic thermocycling of nanoliter droplets via IR laser or LED arrays.

## Feasibility Assessment
Fully proven — multiple groups have demonstrated sub-5-minute PCR. Wittwer achieved 14.7-second PCR in 2015. NextGenPCR sells a 2-minute commercial system.

## Remaining Novel Angles
1. **Sensitivity at ultralow copy** — single-molecule reliability with rapid cycling is flagged as unsolved in 2025 reviews.
2. **Multiplexed photonic PCR** — most systems detect one target.
3. **Active cooling** — passive cooling (7-13°C/s) is 2-3x slower than photonic heating (22°C/s). Peltier-photonic hybrid?
4. **Affordable disposable cartridge** — no commercial photonic PCR product exists despite a decade of academic demonstrations.

## Risk Analysis
The field is mature and crowded. Any submission would need a very specific, novel angle to avoid being "we propose something that already exists."

## Originality Score: 4/10 (well-published field)
## Tractability Score: 9/10 (proven feasible)

## RECOMMENDATION: PRUNE unless a specific novel angle is identified
