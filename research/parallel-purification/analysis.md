# Technical Feasibility Analysis - Acoustic Parallel Purification

## PRUNED

## Reason for Pruning
1. **Fundamental physics barrier**: Acoustic radiation force cannot directly manipulate proteins (too small, ~5-10 nm). Must use carrier particles (beads), making it functionally identical to magnetic bead purification.
2. **Problem is already solved**: His-tag purification takes 4-5 minutes (VFD-IMAC, Capturem). Magnetic bead purification is 5-15 minutes, commercially available, and automated.
3. **No competitive advantage**: Acoustic bead sorting offers no clear speed or purity advantage over magnetic bead separation for protein purification.

## Originality Score: 3/10
## Tractability Score: 4/10 (fundamental ARF limitation)

## Status: PRUNED
